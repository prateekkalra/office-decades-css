Office Storage Through the Decades

Evolution of workplace storage solutions from 1950s to 2020s

1950s - Tall Metal Filing Cabinet
Tall 4:1 vertical ratio cabinet with metallic gray finish, featuring 4-5 stacked drawers with small label holders and centered handles. Classic office storage for paper documents.
1960s - Wooden Desk Drawers
Shorter, wider 2:1 horizontal ratio wooden drawers in warm teak or walnut tones. Features rounded drawer pulls and sits flush under desk surfaces.

2010s - Minimalist Open Shelving
Thin-framed rectangular shelves with no doors - open front and back. Light wood or white finish with neatly stacked plants, folders, and books.
